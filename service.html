services

General Medical Services

- Outpatient Consultations: With specialists in cardiology, dermatology, pediatrics, gynecology, and more.
- Emergency Services: 24/7 urgent care for trauma, acute illness, and stabilization.
- Medical Imaging: X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, and MRIs for diagnostic purposes.
- Laboratory Testing: Blood tests, pathology, and microbiology services.


Specialized Treatments

- Interventional Cardiology: Advanced cardiac procedures such as angioplasty and catheterization.
- Oncology Services: Cancer care including chemotherapy and radiotherapy, often in dedicated oncology centers.
- Surgical Procedures: General surgery, orthopedic surgery, ENT, and more, performed in modern operating theaters.
- Neonatal and Adult Intensive Care: Critical care units for newborns and adults
- requiring intensive monitoring.


Pediatric and Maternity Care

- Pediatric Services: Dedicated child-friendly environments with multidisciplinary teams.
- Obstetrics and Gynecology: Prenatal care, childbirth, and postnatal support.


 Additional Services

- Hospitalization: Comfortable inpatient rooms for short- or long-term stays.
- Rehabilitation and Physiotherapy: Post-surgical recovery and chronic condition management.
- Preventive Medicine: Vaccinations, health screenings, and wellness checkups.
